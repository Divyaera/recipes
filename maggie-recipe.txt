Indredents:
 - Finely chopped onions (Two)
 - Finely chopped tomato (One)
 - 2 tbs oil
 - Green Chillies
 - Green Pea
 - Maggie 
 - Seasonings and lemon to taste

 Directions:
 // While these process keep your flame to sim
 
  1. Preheat the frying pan then add oil to it and wait for 30 seconds, then add finely 
     chopped green chillies to it and stiring it for another one minute. Boil the green
     peas in a boiler at 100 C for 5 minutes.

  3. Now add onions to the frying pan with a pinch of salt and keep stiring it untill 
     the onions get golden brown.

  4. Now add tomatoes and green peas to the frying pan and keep stiring for another 
     2 - 3 minutes and then add 100 ml of water with maggie-masala and mix it well and
     let it cook for another 2 minutes.

  5. Now add maggie to the gravy and mix well and then add 200 ml of water, cover it 
     and let it cook for another 5 minutes.
